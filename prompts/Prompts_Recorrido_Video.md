# 📹 Guía de Prompts para Video Inmobiliario Cinematográfico

Esta guía contiene prompts de alta fidelidad con movimientos de cámara cinematográficos variados. Están diseñados para evitar el efecto de "caminar hacia adelante" y ofrecer una estética de producción profesional.

---

## 🏗️ Cómo usar estos Prompts

1. **Imagen-a-Video:** Sube tu imagen de referencia.
2. **Instrucción Maestra:** Copia esto ANTES de cada prompt para garantizar la consistencia absoluta:
   > **Master Rule:** "STRICTLY MAINTAIN all original materials, architecture, furniture, and decor. ZERO ADDITIONS ALLOWED. Do not invent new objects. Use the source image as the absolute limit."
3. **Selección de Cámara:** Elige un movimiento que aporte dinamismo a la escena.

---

## 🏠 1. Exterior y Fachada

### ORBITA EXTERIOR (Dron Cinematográfico)
> **Prompt:** "Cinematic slow 360-degree orbit around the house facade. Wide-angle 16mm lens feel. Maintain the exact architectural geometry and surrounding landscape. Soft golden hour lighting with realistic lens flares. Zero additions to the garden or building. Stabilized horizontal flight."

### DESCENSO DE GRÚA (Vertical Crane)
> **Prompt:** "Slow vertical crane-down shot starting from the roofline to the main entrance. Emphasize the height and materials. Maintain original trees and ground textures exactly. High dynamic range, crisp shadows, 4k. DO NOT ADD PEOPLE OR VEHICLES."

---

## 🛋️ 2. Sala de Estar y Comedor

### REVELACIÓN LENTA (Dolly Out)
> **Prompt:** "Cinematic slow Dolly-out shot (moving backwards) revealing the scale of the living room. Wide 18mm lens perspective. Maintain all existing furniture positions and rug textures. Natural light streaming through windows with dust motes. No new decor elements added. Stable motion."

### DESLIZAMIENTO LATERAL (Slider Pan)
> **Prompt:** "Smooth horizontal slider movement from right to left across the dining area. Focus on the relationship between furniture and architecture. Maintain original materials and colors. Diffused soft lighting, photorealistic textures. ZERO NEW OBJECTS."

---

## 🍳 3. Cocina

### ENFOQUE SELECTIVO (Macro Rack Focus)
> **Prompt:** "Extreme macro tracking shot along the countertop. Artistic rack focus shifting from a background fixture to the foreground stone texture. Maintain original appliances and materials exactly. Professional studio lighting with sharp reflections. High precision focus, 35mm lens feel."

### DESLIZAMIENTO BAJO (Low Angle Slider)
> **Prompt:** "Low-angle tracking slider moving alongside the kitchen island. Focus on the cabinetry and floor transition. Maintain exact open-plan layout. Balanced indoor/outdoor lighting. Zero modifications to architecture or decor."

---

## 🛌 4. Habitaciones

### MOVIMIENTO DIAGONAL (Diagonal Dolly)
> **Prompt:** "Slow diagonal Dolly-in movement towards the bed corner. 35mm cinematic lens. Maintain original linens, wall colors, and bedside furniture exactly. Gentle volumetric morning light. Peaceful and serene atmosphere. No additional props allowed."

### BARRIDO CENITAL (Top-Down Tilt)
> **Prompt:** "Smooth vertical tilt-down shot starting from the ceiling down to the bed arrangement. Showcase the symmetry of the room. Maintain original textures and layout. Soft diffused lighting, photorealistic. ZERO ADDITIONS."

---

## 🚿 5. Baños

### REVELACIÓN POR ESPEJO (Mirror Pan Reveal)
> **Prompt:** "Slow cinematic pan revealing the bathroom through the vanity mirror reflection. Control reflections to show original fixtures and stone walls only. No added hygiene products. Bright, pristine lighting. 4k resolution, steady gimbal movement."

### DESLIZAMIENTO DE DETALLE (fixture Slider)
> **Prompt:** "Horizontal slider shot focusing on the shower or vanity fixtures. High precision focus on chrome and stone textures. Maintain original materials exactly. Clean professional photography style. ZERO NEW ELEMENTS."

---

## 🌳 6. Patio y Áreas Exteriores

### ORBITA DE PISCINA (Poolside Orbit)
> **Prompt:** "Low-angle circular orbit around the pool edge or patio area. Focus on the reflection of the sky on the water. Maintain original deck materials and landscaping. Sunset lighting (Golden Hour). Do not add plants or outdoor furniture. Hyper-realistic."

---

## 💡 Glosario Técnico (Nuevos Términos)
- **Dolly Out:** Alejar la cámara para revelar más espacio.
- **Crane Down:** Descenso vertical de la cámara (como una grúa).
- **Rack Focus:** Cambio de enfoque de un objeto a otro para guiar la vista.
- **Orbit:** Movimiento circular alrededor de un punto central.
- **Macro:** Plano de muy corta distancia para resaltar texturas.
- **Slider:** Desplazamiento lateral o diagonal muy suave.
