# Prompts de Video - Carnicería El Negro

Estos prompts están diseñados para herramientas de IA de video (Luma Dream Machine, Runway Gen-2/3, Kling, etc.) con el objetivo de maximizar el apetito visual (Food Porn).

---

### 1. Carne al Fuego (`carne y fuego.JPG`)
**Prompt:**
> Cinematic macro shot of thick, juicy meat steaks sizzling on a rustic iron grill. Violent orange flames from the wood fire below lick the edges of the meat. Visible droplets of fat dripping onto the glowing embers, creating tiny bursts of sparks and white smoke. The meat has a perfect charred crust and a glistening, succulent surface. Intense steam rising. High frame rate, slow motion, warm ember lighting, hyper-realistic textures, 4k.

**Versión Cinemagraph (Solo fuego en movimiento):**
> Cinemagraph effect. The meat, grill, and surrounding environment are completely frozen and static. Only the orange flames and sparks from the wood fire move, flickering and dancing realistically while everything else remains as a still photograph.

---

### 2. El Choripán Perfecto (`choripan.JPG`)
**Prompt:**
> Extreme close-up of a succulent choripán held by hands in black professional gloves. The sausage is perfectly grilled, showing a crispy, browned skin that slightly bursts when squeezed, releasing hot juices. Wisps of steam rise from the toasted, crusty bread. The lighting is warm and inviting, highlighting the oily texture of the meat and the soft interior of the bread. Slow motion, commercial food photography style, depth of field, 8k.

**Versión Cinemagraph (Solo vapor en movimiento):**
> Cinemagraph style. The hands in gloves, the bread, and the sausage are captured in a frozen, static moment. Only a delicate, realistic wisp of hot steam rises steadily from the grilled meat, creating a subtle contrast with the perfectly still image.

---

### 3. El Sándwich Maestro (`sanguche carne.JPG`)
**Prompt:**
> Close-up video of a "Carnicería El Negro" pitmaster assembling a massive meat sandwich. The camera focuses on the layers of tender, moist pulled or sliced beef being placed inside a fresh, soft baguette. Real-time steam billows out as the meat is handled. The background shows a professional kitchen environment. High contrast, vibrant colors, emphasizing the moisture and tenderness of the beef. Cinematic lighting, smooth camera movement, ultra-high definition.

**Versión Cinemagraph (Solo vapor en movimiento):**
> Cinemagraph effect. The entire scene, including hands and sandwich, is perfectly frozen and static. Only a very subtle, thin, and translucent wisp of heat steam rises gently from the meat. The movement is barely noticeable and highly realistic, with no fire or thick smoke. Purely delicate steam.

---

**Recomendación general:** Para mejores resultados, sube la imagen original como referencia (Image-to-Video) y usa estos prompts para describir el movimiento y la atmósfera.
